Air pollution, meteorological conditions and respiratory infections in Barcelona (Poblenou)

A 14-year spatiotemporal analysis

This collaboratory project is produced by eight students of 11th grade (first grade baccalaureate), in the subject of coding at Pompeu Fabra High-School (Martorell, Barcelona).

Research Objectives:

  1. To analyze data of respiratory infections and find out possible interactions of air pollutants and meteorological variables in these health conditions.
  2. Data Acquisition: Respiratory infection data are obtained from the Information System for the Surveillance of Infections in Catalonia (SIVIC) .
    Parameters included: Date, epidemiological week, year, region/area, primary care center, diagnosis, sex, age group, socioeconomic index, cases, and population.

    Scope: Our data comes from 66 primary care centers in Barcelona, covering daily data from 2012 to 2025.
