Air pollution, meteorological conditions and respiratory infectionsin Barcelona. A 14-year spatiotemporal analisis

This collaboratory project is produced by 8 students of 11th (first grade baccaulerate), in the subject of coding at Pompeu Fabra High-School (Martorell, Barcelona)

The objectives of this research are:

  1. To analize data of respiratory infections and find out possible interactions of air pollutants and meteorological variables in this health conditions.
  2. First thing to do is to download all data. Respiratory infection data are obtained from Information System for the Surveillance of Infections of Infections in Catalonia (SIVIC).
